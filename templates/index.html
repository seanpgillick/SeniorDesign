ABOUT THIS PROJECT

This crime data portal involves an analysis of crime in the U.S. cities and helps determine safe neighbourhoods

We have used a variety of easy to understand visualizations involving charts, heatmap and a measure called safety score

This project is being developed and mainted by Drexel Students with help of Drexel's faculty members

BENEFITS

Ease of Access

Ability to compare a variety of crime data between the largest U.S. cities

Safety

Safety score will help figure out which U.S. cities are more safe and also we can check safety scores of specific addresses.

Awareness

Spread awareness of crimes occuring in a specific area and see changes in crime levels over the years
